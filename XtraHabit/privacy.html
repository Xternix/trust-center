XtraHabit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

Information We Collect

  • User-provided data: habits, reminders, notes, preferences.
  • Device information: model, OS version, usage logs.
  • Notification data: scheduling and delivery of reminders.
  • Optional account data: email/profile if you sign in via Google, Microsoft, or Apple.

How We Use Information

  • Functionality: habit tracking, reminders, progress visualization.
  • Personalization: improve usability and experience.
  • Reliability: analytics/crash reporting to enhance performance.
  • Notifications: deliver reminders at configured times.

Data Sharing

  • No sale of data: we do not sell personal information.
  • Limited sharing: trusted providers (analytics/crash reporting) as necessary.
  • Authentication: credentials handled by providers; we do not store passwords.

Children’s Privacy

XtraHabit is not directed to children under 13. If under 13, parental consent is required. We comply with applicable child privacy laws.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, restricted access). No method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Delete data: clear app storage or uninstall; request deletion for account-linked data via contact.
  • Opt-out: disable analytics/crash reporting via device settings where supported.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be posted here.
